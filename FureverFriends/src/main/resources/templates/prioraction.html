How to Help Injured Dogs

If you find an injured dog on the street, follow these steps:

  • Approach the dog slowly and calmly to avoid scaring it.
  • Check for visible injuries but avoid touching open wounds.
  • Provide clean water and some soft food if the dog is hungry.
  • Contact a local animal rescue or veterinarian for immediate help.
  • If possible, transport the dog to a vet safely.

How to Help Injured Cats

If you find an injured cat, follow these steps:

  • Observe the cat from a distance to assess its condition.
  • Use a soft cloth or towel to gently pick up the cat if it's safe.
  • Keep the cat warm and quiet in a safe space.
  • Contact a vet or animal rescue service immediately.
  • Do not attempt to force-feed the cat if it appears weak.

How to Help Injured Birds

If you find an injured bird, take these actions:

  • Gently pick up the bird using a soft cloth.
  • Place it in a ventilated box lined with soft fabric.
  • Keep it in a quiet and warm place away from noise.
  • Provide water but do not force-feed.
  • Contact a wildlife rehabilitation center for further help.
